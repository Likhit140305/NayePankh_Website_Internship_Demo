Who we are

A foundation built on belief

NayePankh — meaning "new wings" in Hindi — is a volunteer-driven NGO working to break the cycle of poverty through education and empowerment.

We believe that every child, regardless of background, deserves access to quality education, mentorship, and the tools to build a meaningful future.

📚
Education First
Free tuition, study materials, and academic support for underprivileged students.
💡
Skill Building
Digital literacy, spoken English, and career readiness workshops.
🤝
Community Driven
Everything we do is powered by thousands of passionate volunteers across India.

What we do

Programs that create real change

📖
Free Tuition Initiative
One-on-one and group tutoring sessions for students from Class 1 to 12, taught by our volunteers.
💻
Digital Literacy
Teaching basic computer skills, internet safety, and digital tools to first-generation learners.
🎓
Scholarship Guidance
Helping students discover and apply for government scholarships and financial aid programs.
🗣️
Spoken English
Confidence-building English communication classes for young adults entering the workforce.
♀️
Women Empowerment
Workshops and mentorship programs specifically designed for girls and young women.
🧠
Mental Health Awareness
Awareness campaigns and peer support groups addressing student stress, anxiety, and wellbeing.
