What we do

Programs that create real change

We run volunteer-led programs that directly impact the lives of underprivileged students across India. Every program is free and powered entirely by our community.

📖

Free Tuition Initiative

One-on-one and group tutoring sessions for students from Class 1 to 12, covering all subjects. Taught by our trained volunteers across 18 states.

22,000+ students reached
💻

Digital Literacy

Teaching basic computer skills, internet safety, and digital tools to first-generation learners who have never used a computer.

9,500+ students reached
🎓

Scholarship Guidance

Helping students discover and apply for government scholarships and financial aid programs. We guide on documentation, deadlines, and essay writing.

7,800+ students helped
🗣️

Spoken English

Confidence-building English communication classes for young adults preparing to enter the workforce or higher education.

5,200+ students enrolled
♀️

Women Empowerment

Workshops and mentorship programs specifically designed for girls and young women — covering career, finance, and self-confidence.

3,800+ participants
🧠

Mental Health Awareness

Awareness campaigns and peer support groups addressing student stress, anxiety, and emotional wellbeing through volunteer-led sessions.

1,900+ students supported
